❓ FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions we get.

🚚 How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days across India. Express delivery (1–2 days) available at checkout for select cities.

↩️ What is your return policy?

We offer hassle-free returns within 30 days of delivery. Product must be unused and in original packaging.

🎁 Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes! Free gift wrapping on orders above ₹1,500. Add a personalised message at checkout.

👶 Are toys safe for babies?

All our toys meet EN71 and ASTM safety standards. Age recommendations are listed on each product page.

💳 What payment methods are accepted?

UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, EMI, Wallets via Razorpay — and Cash on Delivery across India.

📦 Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to 25+ countries. International orders use Stripe payment. Shipping fees calculated at checkout.
