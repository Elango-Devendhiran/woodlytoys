⚙️ Step-by-Step

Razorpay Setup Guide

Get your payment gateway live in 6 simple steps — usually done in under 3 business days.

1

Create Account

Go to razorpay.com → Sign Up. Enter your business email and mobile. Verify OTP to create your account.

2

Complete KYC

Submit: PAN card, GST certificate (if any), Bank account details, Aadhaar/Passport, Business registration proof.

3

Get API Keys

After approval → Settings → API Keys → Generate Key. Copy Key ID & Key Secret. Keep the Secret safe!

4

Add to Website

Include Razorpay's JS script in your HTML. Pass your Key ID, order amount, and currency to the checkout handler.

5

Test Payments

Use Razorpay's sandbox test cards to simulate real transactions. Check webhooks are working for order confirmation.

6

Go Live! 🎉

Switch from test keys to live keys. Ensure your site has SSL (HTTPS). You're ready to accept real payments!

📋 Checklist

What You Need to Launch

A complete checklist of everything required to get your Woodly store live and selling.

🌐

Domain Name

e.g., woodlytoys.in — Register on GoDaddy or Namecheap. Costs ₹800–₹1,200 per year.

🖥️

Web Hosting + SSL

Hostinger or AWS. SSL certificate is mandatory for payment gateways. Cost: ₹2,000–₹6,000/year.

🏦

Business Bank Account

A current account in your business name is required for payment gateway settlement and KYC.

📄

GST Registration

Required if annual turnover exceeds ₹20 lakhs. Apply at gst.gov.in. Takes 3–7 working days.

💳

Razorpay / Stripe Account

Sign up, complete KYC, and get API keys. Approval takes 2–5 business days for Indian accounts.

🚚

Shipping Partner

Shiprocket, Delhivery, or BlueDart for pan-India delivery with real-time tracking for customers.

📜

Legal Pages

Privacy Policy, Return Policy, Terms & Conditions — required by payment gateways and e-commerce law.

📸

Product Photography

High-quality photos per the image guide below. Use natural light — DSLR or iPhone 12+ works well.
