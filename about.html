🌱 Our Beginning

Born in a Backyard Workshop in 2018

Master carpenter Rajan Kumar started Woodly after his daughter's plastic toys broke within days. Frustrated by poor quality, he crafted her a wooden puzzle in his Ooty workshop — and she was enchanted.

Neighbours noticed. Parents wanted more. Word spread across the Nilgiris. What started as a one-man passion project grew into a team of 40 skilled artisans, producing 137+ unique wooden toys shipped across India.

Every toy we make carries the same principle: built to last a childhood, built to be loved.

💚 What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Principles that guide every toy we make and every choice we take.

🌿

Sustainability First

FSC-certified wood only. Every tree used is replanted. We are committed to a greener planet for the children we serve.

🛡️

Safety Above All

Every toy meets EN71 & ASTM international safety standards. Non-toxic paints, smooth edges, child-safe finishes.

🔨

Artisan Craftsmanship

No assembly lines. Each piece is hand-carved, hand-painted, and quality-checked by a real person before it reaches you.

🧠

Learning Through Play

Every toy is designed with child development in mind — fine motor skills, creativity, problem-solving, and sensory growth.

🤝

Community Impact

We employ local artisans at fair wages, support women-led craft groups, and donate 2% of profits to children's education.

💌

Joy in Every Box

Hand-written notes, eco-friendly packaging, and free gift wrapping — because unpacking should be as magical as playing.
